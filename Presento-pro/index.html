Who We Are?

GM Tjiane Attorneys is a level 1 BBBEE law firm based in Pretoria. We provide tailored and integrated legal services across a wide range of practice areas. The firm operates under a youthful dynamic team of lawyers that understand the core issues of the average South African and are committed to using their wealth of knowledge and exceptional expertise to bring them the best possible solutions. The firm’s existence is hinged and anchored on providing legal assistance to the marginalised and disfranchised individuals in need of legal recourse. We uphold the highest standards of integrity, with an emphasis on seeking the most satisfactory solution for our clients with great speed and efficiency.
